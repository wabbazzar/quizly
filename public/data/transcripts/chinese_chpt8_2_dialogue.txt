Letters and Abilities
Estimated listening time: 14 minutes

封, measure word for letters.
信, letter.
最近, recently.
学期, semester.

小明: 你最近收到几封信？
小美: 我这个学期收到了三封信。

Xiaoming: How many letters have you received recently?
Xiaomei: I received three letters this semester.

小明: 你最近收到几封信？
小美: 我这个学期收到了三封信。

最, most.
近, close.
专业, major.
会, can.

小美: 我学中文。我会说一点中文。

Xiaoming: What's your major?
Xiaomei: I study Chinese. I can speak a little Chinese.

小明: 你的专业是什么？
小美: 我学中文。我会说一点中文。
