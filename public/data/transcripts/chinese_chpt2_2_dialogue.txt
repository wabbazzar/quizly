Chinese Dialogue Practice - Chapter 2
Family and Occupations
Estimated listening time: 10 minutes

家, family; home.
几, how many.
口, measure word for family members.
人, person.

小明: 小美，你家有几口人？
小美: 我家有五口人。你呢？

Xiaoming: Xiaomei, how many people are in your family?
Xiaomei: There are five people in my family. What about you?

哥哥, older brother.
妹妹, younger sister.
两, two.
和, and.

小明: 我家有四口人。我有一个哥哥。你有哥哥吗？
小美: 我没有哥哥，可是我有两个妹妹。

Xiaoming: There are four people in my family. I have an older brother. Do you have an older brother?
Xiaomei: I don't have an older brother, but I have two younger sisters.

大姐, eldest sister.
二姐, second oldest sister.
都, both; all.
大, big.

小明: 你有姐姐吗？
小美: 有，我有大姐和二姐。她们都很大了。

Xiaoming: Do you have older sisters?
Xiaomei: Yes, I have an eldest sister and a second sister. They are both grown up now.

工作, job; to work.
做, to do.
什么, what.
律师, lawyer.

小明: 你大姐做什么工作？
小美: 我大姐是律师。她的工作很忙。

Xiaoming: What does your eldest sister do for work?
Xiaomei: My eldest sister is a lawyer. Her job is very busy.

医生, doctor.
二姐, second sister.
也, also.
很, very.

小明: 你二姐呢？她也是律师吗？
小美: 不是，我二姐是医生。她也很忙。

Xiaoming: What about your second sister? Is she also a lawyer?
Xiaomei: No, my second sister is a doctor. She's also very busy.

大学生, college student.
大学, university.
学生, student.
两, two.

小明: 你的两个妹妹是学生吗？
小美: 是的，她们都是大学生。她们在北京上大学。

Xiaoming: Are your two younger sisters students?
Xiaomei: Yes, they are both college students. They go to university in Beijing.

英文, English language.
学, to study.
老师, teacher.
工作, job.

小明: 你妹妹学什么？
小美: 大妹妹学英文，小妹妹想当老师。

Xiaoming: What do your sisters study?
Xiaomei: My older younger sister studies English, my youngest sister wants to be a teacher.

哥哥, older brother.
做, to do.
工作, to work.
律师, lawyer.

小美: 你哥哥做什么工作？
小明: 我哥哥也是律师。他在纽约工作。

Xiaomei: What does your older brother do for work?
Xiaoming: My older brother is also a lawyer. He works in New York.

家, family.
都, all.
忙, busy.
很, very.

小美: 你们家人都很忙吧？
小明: 是的，我们家人都很忙。可是周末我们都在家。

Xiaomei: Your family members are all very busy, right?
Xiaoming: Yes, our family is all very busy. But on weekends we're all at home.

大学, university.
学生, student.
美国, America.
中国, China.

小明: 我在美国上大学。你在中国上大学吗？
小美: 是的，我在北京上大学。我是大学生。

Xiaoming: I go to university in America. Do you go to university in China?
Xiaomei: Yes, I go to university in Beijing. I'm a college student.

医生, doctor.
律师, lawyer.
工作, job.
好, good.

小明: 你想做医生还是律师？
小美: 我想做老师。我觉得老师的工作很好。

Xiaoming: Do you want to be a doctor or a lawyer?
Xiaomei: I want to be a teacher. I think being a teacher is a good job.

END OF DIALOGUE
