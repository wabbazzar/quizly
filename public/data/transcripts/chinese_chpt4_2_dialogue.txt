Chinese Dialogue Practice - Chapter 4
Weekend Plans & Entertainment
Estimated listening time: 10 minutes

周末, weekend.
打球, to play ball.
电视, television.
看, to watch.

小明: 周末你想做什么？
小美: 我不想打球，我想在家看电视。

Xiaoming: What do you want to do this weekend?
Xiaomei: I don't want to play ball, I want to stay home and watch TV.

电影, movie.
外国, foreign country.
有意思, interesting.
觉得, to feel; to think.

小明: 看电视？你喜欢看什么？
小美: 我喜欢看外国电影。我觉得外国电影很有意思。

Xiaoming: Watch TV? What do you like to watch?
Xiaomei: I like watching foreign movies. I think foreign movies are interesting.

音乐, music.
听, to listen.
唱歌, to sing.
跳舞, to dance.

小明: 我也觉得电影有意思。你喜欢听音乐吗？
小美: 喜欢！我常常听音乐，有时候还唱歌跳舞。

Xiaoming: I also think movies are interesting. Do you like listening to music?
Xiaomei: Yes! I often listen to music, and sometimes I even sing and dance.

常常, often.
时候, time; moment.
有的, some.
书, book.

小明: 有的时候我也听音乐。你喜欢看书吗？
小美: 我有的时候看书，可是我不常常看。

Xiaoming: Sometimes I also listen to music. Do you like reading books?
Xiaomei: I sometimes read books, but I don't often read.

对, right; correct.
想, to want to; to think.
那, in that case; then.
去, to go.

小明: 对，我也不常常看书。那周末我们去看电影吧？
小美: 好主意！我想看那个新电影。

Xiaoming: Right, I don't often read books either. Then let's go watch a movie this weekend?
Xiaomei: Good idea! I want to see that new movie.

好久, a long time.
昨天, yesterday.
请客, to treat someone.
所以, so.

小明: 我好久没看电影了。昨天我朋友请客，所以我很高兴。
小美: 你朋友请你吃饭了？真好！

Xiaoming: I haven't watched a movie for a long time. Yesterday my friend treated me, so I was very happy.
Xiaomei: Your friend treated you to dinner? How nice!

不错, pretty good.
错, wrong.
小, small; little.
别人, other people.

小明: 对，那个饭店不错。小美，你觉得这个电影院怎么样？
小美: 这个电影院有点儿小，可是别人都说很好。

Xiaoming: Yes, that restaurant was pretty good. Xiaomei, what do you think of this movie theater?
Xiaomei: This movie theater is a bit small, but other people all say it's good.

久, long (of time).
只, only.
睡觉, to sleep.
算了, forget it; never mind.

小美: 可是电影很长，要看两个小时。
小明: 没关系。我昨天只睡觉了，今天不累。

Xiaomei: But the movie is long, it takes two hours to watch.
Xiaoming: No problem. I only slept yesterday, so I'm not tired today.

找, to look for.
别的, other.
好, very.
意思, meaning.

小美: 那我们找找电影票吧。有没有别的好电影？
小明: 让我看看。这个电影的名字好有意思。

Xiaomei: Then let's look for movie tickets. Are there any other good movies?
Xiaoming: Let me see. The name of this movie is very interesting.

电影, movie.
看, to watch.
周末, weekend.
有意思, interesting.

小明: 好，我们就看这个电影。
小美: 太好了！这个周末会很有意思。

Xiaoming: Okay, let's watch this movie.
Xiaomei: Great! This weekend will be very interesting.

END OF DIALOGUE
