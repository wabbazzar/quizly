Chinese Dialogue Practice - Chapter 7
Daily Routines and Classes
Estimated listening time: 14 minutes

平常, usually.
早, early.
这么, so.
晚, late.

小明: 你平常几点起床？
小美: 我平常六点起床。今天起得这么晚！

Xiaoming: What time do you usually wake up?
Xiaomei: I usually wake up at six. I woke up so late today!

小明: 你平常几点起床？
小美: 我平常六点起床。今天起得这么晚！

Xiaoming: What do you do in the morning?
Xiaomei: In the morning I do homework, then go to class. Everybody is busy.

小明: 早上你做什么？
小美: 早上我做功课，然后上课。大家都很忙。

小明: 上课什么时候开始？
