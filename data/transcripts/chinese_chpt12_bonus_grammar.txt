Pattern Two. Using duo and shao before verbs.
少放盐，多放点儿糖。
Add less salt and more sugar.

Pattern Three. Gang versus gangcai.
他刚从中国来。
He just came from China.
他刚才在这儿。
He was here a moment ago.

Pattern Four. Resultative complements.
小白菜卖完了。
Baby bok choy is sold out.

Pattern Five. Hao as a resultative complement.
饭做好了。
The food is ready.
