Chinese Dialogue Practice - Common Review
An Evening at Home - The Full Story
Estimated listening time: 10 minutes

下班, to get off work.
回家, to go home.
天气, weather.
冷, cold.

小美: 老公，我下班了，在回家的路上。今天天气好冷啊！
小明: 路上小心！我在家等你。要不要我去接你？
小美: 不用了，我快到了。家里暖和吗？
小明: 暖和！我把暖气开了。猫也在门口等你呢。

Xiaomei: Honey, I'm off work and on my way home. The weather is so cold today!
Xiaoming: Be careful! I'm waiting at home. Want me to come get you?
Xiaomei: No need, I'm almost there. Is it warm inside?
Xiaoming: Warm! I turned on the heater. The cat is waiting for you at the door too.

到家, arrive home.
累, tired.
上班, to go to work.
告诉, to tell.

小美: 我到家了！好累啊，今天上班忙死了。
小明: 快进来！鞋子换了，坐下来休息。告诉我，今天怎么了？
小美: 开了三个会，写了两个报告。你呢？你今天做什么了？
小明: 我今天没上班，在家工作的。早上去跑步了，然后就在家。

Xiaomei: I'm home! So tired — work was crazy busy today.
Xiaoming: Come in! Change your shoes and sit down to rest. Tell me, what happened today?
Xiaomei: Had three meetings, wrote two reports. How about you? What did you do today?
Xiaoming: I didn't go to the office today — worked from home. Went running in the morning, then stayed home.

猫, cat.
喜欢, to like.
开心, happy.
真, really.

小美: 猫咪！你想我了吗？来，让我抱抱你。
小明: 她今天一直在睡觉。你一回来她就开心了。
小美: 她真的很喜欢我，对不对？
小明: 她也喜欢我！但是你不在的时候，她就在你的椅子上睡觉。
小美: 真可爱。你今天给她吃猫粮了吗？
小明: 给了。早上和中午都给了。

Xiaomei: Kitty! Did you miss me? Come, let me hold you.
Xiaoming: She was sleeping all day. She got happy as soon as you came home.
Xiaomei: She really likes me, right?
Xiaoming: She likes me too! But when you're not here, she sleeps on your chair.
Xiaomei: So cute. Did you feed her today?
Xiaoming: Yes. Fed her in the morning and at noon.

吃, to eat.
做饭, to cook.
外卖, takeout.
想, to want.

小明: 你想吃什么？我做饭还是我们点外卖？
小美: 我太累了，不想做饭。你能做吗？
小明: 能啊！你想吃什么菜？
小美: 随便。你做什么我就吃什么。
小明: 那我做面条吧。简单又好吃。
小美: 好的！你做饭，我去洗个澡。

Xiaoming: What do you want to eat? Should I cook or should we order delivery?
Xiaomei: I'm too tired — don't want to cook. Can you?
Xiaoming: Sure! What do you want?
Xiaomei: Anything. I'll eat whatever you make.
Xiaoming: Then I'll make noodles. Simple and delicious.
Xiaomei: Okay! You cook, I'll go take a shower.

咖啡, coffee.
知道, to know.
说, to say.
什么, what.

小美: 面条好好吃！你什么时候学会做面条的？
小明: 上个星期在网上看了一个视频。你知道吗，其实很容易做。
小美: 你要喝什么？我去泡茶还是咖啡？
小明: 晚上了，不喝咖啡了。泡茶吧。
小美: 好。有人跟我说一个新的茶很好喝，我买了一盒。
小明: 好啊，我们试试。

Xiaomei: The noodles are so good! When did you learn to make them?
Xiaoming: Last week I watched a video online. You know, they're actually easy to make.

Xiaoming: It's late — no coffee. Let's have tea.
Xiaomei: Okay. Someone told me about a new tea that's good — I bought a box.
Xiaoming: Great, let's try it.

锻炼, to exercise.
跑步, to run.
散步, to take a walk.
明天, tomorrow.

小明: 吃完了。我们去外面散步吧？
小美: 好啊，但是外面很冷。我要多穿一点。
小明: 没问题。我给你拿外套。
小美: 你今天跑步跑了多远？
小明: 五公里。明天你想一起去锻炼吗？
小美: 想！但是我跑不了五公里，我先跑两公里吧。
小明: 好，我们明天一起去。

Xiaoming: Done eating. Should we go for a walk outside?
Xiaomei: Sure, but it's cold outside. I need to wear more layers.
Xiaoming: No problem. I'll get your jacket.
Xiaomei: How far did you run today?
Xiaoming: Five kilometers. Want to exercise together tomorrow?
Xiaomei: Yes! But I can't run five kilometers — I'll start with two.
Xiaoming: Okay, let's go together tomorrow.

电视剧, TV show.
看, to watch.
无聊, bored.

小明: 你想看什么？

小明: 我在看一个新的美剧。挺好看的，你要不要一起看？
小美: 好！是什么电视剧？
小明: 你看了就知道了。来，沙发上坐好，我去拿毯子。
小美: 猫也来了！她也要一起看电视。

Xiaomei: Back from the walk. Let's watch TV.

Xiaomei: Sure! What show is it?
Xiaoming: You'll see. Come, sit on the couch — I'll get the blanket.

睡觉, to sleep.
开心, happy.
很, very.
好, good.

小明: 已经很晚了，我们睡觉吧。
小美: 今天虽然累，但是很开心。跟你在一起我就很开心。
小明: 我也是。明天我做早饭，你多睡一会儿。
小美: 你真好。谢谢你今天做饭，谢谢你和我散步。
小明: 不用谢。猫已经在床上了，她比我们先去睡了。
小美: 哈哈，好吧。晚安，老公。
小明: 晚安，老婆。明天见！

Xiaoming: It's getting late — let's go to sleep.

Xiaomei: You're so sweet. Thank you for cooking today, and for walking with me.
Xiaoming: No need to thank me. The cat is already on the bed — she went to sleep before us.
Xiaomei: Ha ha, okay. Good night, honey.
Xiaoming: Good night, honey. See you tomorrow!
